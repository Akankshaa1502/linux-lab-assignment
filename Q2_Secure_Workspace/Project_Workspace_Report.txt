=== How Permissions Protect Project Data ===
Restricting ProjectWorkspace to 750 ensures only the owner has full read/write/execute access, the group can view and enter but not modify, and all other users are completely blocked. Correct ownership (codespace:codespace) ensures the right user/group controls the files. Together, this prevents unauthorized users from reading, altering, or deleting sensitive project files while still allowing legitimate collaborators (the group) limited access.
